work

cineman

Cineman helps users plan a perfect night-in by finding a combination of shows and restaurants based on their chosen criteria (genre, streaming service, cuisine, location).

code quiz

The purpose of this project is to test coding bootcamp students on their knowledge of coding, specifically HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Users are timed and can input their high scores when they are done.

weather dashboard

This project allows users to search for a city and get weather conditions of that city. Users can also access weather conditions of past cities that they have searched for by clicking on the buttons in the Past Searches list.
